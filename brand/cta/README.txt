YOUR CTA OUTRO  (for /highlight "mid" videos)
=============================================

Drop a short outro clip here named:  outro.mp4

What it's for
-------------
When /highlight turns a long recording into ranked horizontal (16:9) "mid" videos
for your channel, it can append YOUR call-to-action outro to the end of every mid
(e.g. "subscribe", "grab the free guide at <your-link>", "book a call").

How it works
------------
- Put your outro clip here as  brand/cta/outro.mp4  (16:9, same look you want on every mid).
- /highlight will automatically append it to each mid it builds.
- No file here? No problem — it just skips the outro. It's optional, never required.

Tip: keep it short (5-15s) and end on your single clearest ask.
